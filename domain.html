Chicks need special care and management for their survival. Farmers always concern about the huddling and dying of chicks inside the brooder. Predator intrusion can affect chicks in two ways as direct predator attack and sudden huddling due to scaring. Generally, during a predator attack, a considerable amount of chicks will die and due to scaring, chicks tend to huddle and from that also chicks will die.

The average hen produces an egg every 24 to 26 hours and “takes a rest” from egg production when the days are short. Taking these two factors into consideration, the average hen will produce about 265 eggs in a year. Most hens have two productive years of egg-laying and taper off as they age. Therefore, the average hen will lay about 530 eggs throughout her lifetime [20]. Therefore it is important to prevent or get informed a predator attack as soon as possible in order to save at least one life. Minimizing deaths will directly affect for the farmers profit.

Main Objective

Main objective of this research is to build a device for the brooder and a mobile application for the farmers giving all privileges to control the device through mobile application.

Specific Objectives

▪ bird-eye view images of the brooder.

▪ Detect if there is any predator inside the brooder.

▪ Detect if there is any sudden huddle inside the brooder.

▪ Send a mobile alert if any predator is detected with a live photograph..

▪ Implement a function to check the live view of the brooder using an image.

▪ Read environment parameters (Temperature, Light intensity) using sensor modules

▪ Control light panel and IR heat bulbs

▪ Count all the chicks in the brooder and send the count of the live chicks

▪ Count of dead chicks and count of all the chicks in the brooder to the farmer.

▪ Detect the pH levels, water levels and moisture levels





This system consists of 2 main components as the main device and mobile application. The main device includes a raspberry pi computer and an Arduino board. The main software run on the Rasbian operating system developed in python. A camera is connected to the raspberry pi computer to take images of the brooder and the artificial intelligence (AI) processing part is run on the raspberry pi computer. Light, pH, moisture, and temperature sensors are connected to the Arduino board. Arduino board is used to control the relay module and read all the sensor data. serial monitoring technology is used to communicate between raspberry pi and Arduino board.
